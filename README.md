# Executive Product Signal Center

## Purpose
This repository curates the most relevant signals from product, AI, and strategy leaders to inform executive-level thinking and decision-making.

It transforms information into structured insight.

---

## What This Repo Is

- A curated intelligence system
- A structured collection of high-quality sources
- A signal extraction and synthesis layer
- A guide to staying current in product leadership

---

## What This Repo Is NOT

- A list of random bookmarks
- A passive collection of links
- A content archive without structure
- A replacement for critical thinking

---

## Who This Is For

- Executive product leaders
- Product strategists
- Operators who need to stay current on trends

---

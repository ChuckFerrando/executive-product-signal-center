# AI Taste at Speed Signal

## Source
Aakash Gupta — “Taste at Speed” (2026)

## Signal Summary

AI has dramatically reduced the cost and time required to build product prototypes, shifting the bottleneck from execution to evaluation.

## Key Observations

- Prototyping can now happen in minutes instead of weeks
- Traditional processes (e.g., PRDs) are becoming less central
- The product workflow is shifting to:
  - build → evaluate → iterate
- High-performing teams:
  - generate many variations
  - kill the majority (~80%)
  - select the best options
- The limiting factor is no longer speed of execution, but quality of judgment

## Signal Implications

- Execution is commoditized
- Evaluation becomes the core differentiator
- Product leaders must develop strong filtering capability
- Poor judgment leads to faster failure at scale

## Classification

- Type: Product + Capability Shift
- Domain: AI Product Leadership
- Strength: Very High
- Actionability: Very High
